title: Tags on Epics, Stories & Tasks
updated: '2026-03-01'
---

Add tagging support to epics, stories, and tasks. In hub projects, tags like "API", "Frontend", "Backend" help categorize work by system or domain. In large hub projects with multiple systems, tags like "API1", "API2" let teams identify which specific system a work item relates to. Tags should be filterable on the board and in search, enabling teams to quickly find all work related to a particular system or concern.
